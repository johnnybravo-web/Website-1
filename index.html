    ABOUT US

    T4TEQ Software Solutions is a software development and training company that was founded in 2017 in Tiruchirappalli, Tamil Nadu, India. It began as a small training company, but it was incorporated in 2019 under the Ministry of Corporate Affairs, Govt. of India as a corporate training and development company. It is internationally accredited as an ISO 9001:2015 certified training company by System and Product Certification, UK.


    T4TEQ Software Solutions was honoured for its outstanding achievements in training and education by TPL Techno Awards, a prominent organization that recognizes excellence in the field of technology. The award was presented to T4TEQ Software Solutions as a “Best Training Company” at a ceremony held in Dwarka, New Delhi. This award is a testament to T4TEQ Software Solutions' dedication to providing high-quality training and education to its students and clients.


    T4TEQ Software Solutions was founded with the mission of training, developing, and providing career guidance to students. It aims to produce skilled and well-rounded employees for the IT and ITES industries. T4TEQ offers end-to-end training programs in programming, soft skills, and interview training, as well as collaborative software projects and real-time scenarios for corporates and educational institutions.
